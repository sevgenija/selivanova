Something about me

Hi! My name is Evgenia Selivanova. At the moment I am getting my master degree in New Media Design (MS Communication and Information Sciences, New Media Design Track) at Tilburg University in the Netherlands. I am a fan of clean pixel-perfect design understandable for users, new technologies and coding. Additionally, I am well familiar with communication research methodology and statistics, can execute difficult projects based on scientific approach. My areas of interest include: UX design, web design and web development, data driven design, prototyping, usability and UX evaluation, applied data visualisation.

What I like

In my free time I enjoy travelling and exploring new places. I am a big fan of nature and am always in for all the nature related activities, such as hiking, kayaking or bouldering. I love sports and whenever I have time I try to go out for a nice run in a park. I still believe that nothing is better than an interesting book, so I like spending my evenings reading or sometimes watching a cool movie.
