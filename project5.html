These are two prototypes of the app, which helps users in every day life by teaching them how to match clothes. Instead of spending hours by standing in front of the mirror and picking the right combination, play a fun game based on the current trends, get the score and increase your clothing matching knowledge. The game uses gamification elements such as points and badges and enables users to compete with other comunity members. The first game prototype was created on initial stage to only show how the dragging and placing function could work. The mockups have been created with Photoshop and Figma. The code for the game was written in HTML, CSS3 and JQuery.
